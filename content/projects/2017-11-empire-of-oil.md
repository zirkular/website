EMPIRE OF OIL 2017-18 | Costa Compagnie

Empire of Oil explores the ambiguity of the oil industry by comparing Norway and Northern Irak, Kurdistan. Wealth, social security, prosperity, war, death and the destruction of our planet intersect. Being surrounded by a 360°-Video-Horizon the audience is thrown into the inescapable reality of this empire.

Between November 2017 and May 2018, the group will develop three intermedial performances and a virtual reality film, based on 360°-video material filmed on-site in Stavanger, Bergen, Kristiansand, Erbil, Sulaymaniyah, Kirkuk and Mosul, the latter during the Kurdish referendum and closure of the air-space.
